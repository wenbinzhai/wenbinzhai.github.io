Professional Summary

Ph.D. student in the Department of Computing at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, with research focused on LLM agent security. My work studies the security of tool-augmented agents, agentic workflows, and external agent components such as tools, memory, and retrieval systems. I am broadly interested in cybersecurity, trustworthy AI, and secure AI agent systems.

Research Interests

Research Areas: LLM Agent Security, Trustworthy AI, Cybersecurity, Wireless Sensor Networks
